Ryan Wang

I am an incoming PhD student at UC Berkeley, where I’ll be advised by Sewon Min as part of the Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) and the Berkeley NLP Group. I am supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Previously, I did my undergrad at the University of Southern California, where I had a wonderful time working with my advisors, Robin Jia and Swabha Swayamdipta.

I'm generally interested in investigating data and architectural design choices during model pre-training that can make models controllable and reliable.

Before NLP, I dabbled with a little bit of computational biology research.
